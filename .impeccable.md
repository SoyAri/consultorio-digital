# Design Context

### Users
- **Patients**: Looking for dental services, trust, and professional care.
- **Dental Team**: Accessing their dashboard.
- **Context**: Often anxious or looking for specific aesthetic/health solutions.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Clinical yet compassionate.
- **Tone**: Precise, ethical, premium.
- **Personality**: Professional, clean, avant-garde.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Clean, medical-premium, tech-forward.
- **Theme**: Light/Medical but with high-impact accents.
- **Primary Color**: Teal (#0F766E).
- **Typography**: 
  - Headings: Lexend
  - Body: Source Sans 3

### Design Principles
1. **Clinical Precision**: Sharp lines, clear hierarchy, and meticulous alignment.
2. **Organic Flow**: Use of curves (like a tooth/smile) and smooth transitions.
3. **High Impact**: Use of 3D elements and cinematic reveals to stand out.
4. **Accessible Professionalism**: WCAG AA contrast and clear call-to-actions.
